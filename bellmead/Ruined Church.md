#### The Nave
- Long, narrow, roof mostly collapsed    
- A **massive, twisted tree** grows up through the cracked stone floor at the center    
- Its branches punch through the upper structure and entwine with the **Choir Loft**    
- Light falls in angled shafts, catching drifting dust, ash, and pollen    
- Pews are broken, stacked, or half-swallowed by roots
The tree is not decorative.  
It is **a consequence**.

The a dissonant song lilts through the Nave, seemingly coming from the Choir Loft, but it's hard to tell.

Subs:
* Tree
