A small glass lantern, blackened with soot. Though there is no flame, a faint glow stirs when grief is near. Each night Liorna carried it to the Bell Tower as a beacon for the one she loved. The flamed guttered the night she died, but her devotion never ceased.

"I lit it so he would know I never stopped looking."

Can be lit with a candle, to act as a lantern in low light.

{% if h != 'hide' %}

Echo of Silent Vigil can be used to have it light the way to the hidden chamber beneath the Bell Tower.
Can be combined with other Echoes for other effects.
