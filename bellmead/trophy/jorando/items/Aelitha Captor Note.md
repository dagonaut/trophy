This is the note found on the body in the Belltower Hidden Chamber. It is a confession.

I did not want to be there. They said they needed volunteers, so no one could claim ignorance. I said I was there just as a witness... 

She said nothing when we came for her. Just continued her work by the candlelight.  As we pulled her away the flame bent toward her like it wanted to follow. She looked back as if to apologize for leaving things unfinished. When the door closed she was still looking back. We turned the lock, lowered the metal plate, and tried to forget. They gave me piece of the mechanism for safe keeping, the only one to remember. I came here looking for the rest... not that I would know what to do.

The Well started weeping that night and continues still. The others don't hear it, but I do. Try as they might, they could not silence her.

I don't know how long its been. When you can't sleep you can't count the days. I no longer know if the sobs are hers or mine. That ends tonight... and that damned candle still burns.
