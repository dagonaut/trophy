**Hammer of the Last Toll**

_The shattered hammer from Evershade’s bell, its haft splintered and wrapped in old cloth. The metal head is scarred and misshapen, as though it struck something other than bronze. When lifted, it carries a faint vibration, like a sound waiting to be finished._

> _“The bell rang once for warning, once for mourning. The third toll was never meant to be heard.”_

---

**Effect:**  
When you strike a foe or object with the Bell-Hammer, you may **declare a Toll**.

- **First Toll:** Break something fragile, false, or concealed. Illusions falter, hidden doors shudder, bindings weaken.
    
- **Second Toll:** Stagger a powerful enemy or interrupt a ritual, miracle, or spell in progress.
    
- **Third Toll:** Deal devastating harm, but immediately take **1 Ruin** as the hammer answers back with sorrow.
    

Each Toll may be used once per delve. When all three are spent, the hammer grows cold and silent until returned to a place of mourning.
