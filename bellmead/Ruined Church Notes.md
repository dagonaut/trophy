## 2. The “Office” Reframed (Evocative Names)

Here are several options depending on tone emphasis. I recommend picking one and committing hard.

### Primary Recommendation

### **The Chamber of Absolution**

**Purpose (historical):**

- Saints recorded confessions.
    
- Weighed grief.
    
- Decided who was “ready” for the lake and the cistern.
    

**Current State:**

- Confessional screens rotted away.
    
- Stone desks carved with tally marks.
    
- Basins stained with dried salt and tear residue.
    
- Chains in the floor where pilgrims were restrained during rites.
    

**Why it works:**

- Naturally foreshadows the Reliquary and the Cistern.

You want something inevitable but earned.

- Saints’ heads turn.

**Opening Line (choral, not shouted):**

This gives you pacing control.
