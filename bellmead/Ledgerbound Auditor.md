### A Haunting Event
#### The Ledgerbound Auditor (Echo)

**What It Is:**

- An Echo formed from the collective act of recording grief.
    
- It is not hostile by default.
    
- It exists to _balance the books_.
    

**Appearance (for image later):**

- A hunched, semi-formed figure emerging from piled ledgers.
    
- Pages flutter where a face should be.
    
- Ink drips upward into the air.
    
- Chains trail from its wrists into the floor.
    

**Behavior:**

- Repeats fragments of confession.
    
- Asks questions like:
    
    - “Is this carried, or abandoned?”
        
    - “Who bears this now?”
        
- Reacts strongly to dishonesty.
    

**Function in the Ritual (Crypt Door):**

- The Echo must be **consulted** or **satisfied**.
    
- It verifies whether grief has been:
    
    - weighed,
        
    - acknowledged,
        
    - and prepared for release.
        

**Mechanical Hook (light, Trophy-friendly):**

- If treated respectfully, it grants insight or lowers risk.
    
- If ignored or attacked, it marks the party with a lingering burden.
